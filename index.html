Background Stories

I picked up Bagheera and Leo from a pet store while I was living in Deerfield Beach, FL in 2016. I had moved there the year before and was ready to have some companions. They were both 4 months old when I got them and now they are already 6! Time really does fly.

Mowgli has quite a different story. I recently moved back to Atlanta, GA and was out running errands before leaving for a vacation a days later. As I was driving all of the sudden I saw this black thing in the middle of the road that everyone was swerving around. That is when I realized it was a baby kitten. No one else was stopping and I realized there was no way I could go the rest of my day not knowing if it survived or not. So I pulled over into the next parking lot, jumped out of my car and ran out into the road to grab it. Next thing I know he has stolen my heart and I realize I have to keep him after saving his life. He went through a few name changes before I finally settled on Mowgli.

Leo

Leo is also very snuggly and shy, but also very talkative. He LOVES to play and would play all day long if I had the time to entertain him that long. His favorite toys are Mardi Gras beads, shoe strings, the lazor pointer, and toy mouses. Basically any toy that makes a sound he loves it. He also loves to rip paper so if there is a paper towel left behind, it will be shreaded to bits.

Mowgli

Mowgli is my fiesty one! He only snuggles when he thinks I don't notice. He is independent and LOVES to be outside. He will spend all day out on the patio if I let him. Which can be brutal in the summer and winter months, but I know it makes him happy. He loves watching the birds, squirrals, and leaves that blow in the wind. He loves to play fetch with his mouse toy and he plays soccer with his toy balls. I believe he is a bit of a dog at heart and was a soccer player in a past life.
